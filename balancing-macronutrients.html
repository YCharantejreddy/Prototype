Balancing Macronutrients

What Are Macronutrients?

Macronutrients—proteins, carbohydrates, and fats—are the primary nutrients your body needs in large amounts for energy and growth. According to the USDA, a balanced diet typically includes:

  • Proteins: 10-35% of daily calories (builds muscle, repairs tissues).
  • Carbohydrates: 45-65% of daily calories (main energy source).
  • Fats: 20-35% of daily calories (supports cell function, hormone production).

Balancing for Your Goals

Adjust macronutrient ratios based on your objectives (source: NIH):

  • Muscle Building: 25-30% protein, 40-50% carbs, 20-25% fats.
  • Weight Loss: 20-25% protein, 40-45% carbs, 25-30% fats.
  • Endurance: 15-20% protein, 55-65% carbs, 20-25% fats.
