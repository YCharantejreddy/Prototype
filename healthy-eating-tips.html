Healthy Eating Tips

Top Tips for a Healthier Diet

Based on recommendations from the American Heart Association and WHO:

  • Eat a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables (5+ servings daily).
  • Opt for whole grains (e.g., brown rice, quinoa) over refined grains.
  • Limit added sugars to less than 10% of daily calories.
  • Choose healthy fats (e.g., avocados, nuts) over saturated fats.
  • Stay hydrated with 8-10 cups of water daily.
